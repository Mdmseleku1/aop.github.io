FAQ


On this page you can find answers to some of the most common questions about the website and the service we offer. Please be advised that this page may not contain every possible answer to every possible scenario or problem. If you don't see what you need then please use the contact form on the "Contact Us" page


How do I create an account and do I have to enter credit card information?

In order to create an account you must visit the "Register" page above. Once there you will be asked to complete all the information on the form provided. Please be advised that all the form fields are required and the form cannot be submitted until all fields are filled in. This is necessary for you to be able to interact with the website in more ways than just vieweing content. 

Creating an account is completely free and we will not ask you for any kind of payment information. If you are redirected to a page that asks you for any such information then please leave that page immediately as that is not our doing and may be a scam to gather personal information.

What do I do if I forget my credentials ?

If you have forgotten your password then simply click on the link provided below the login form on the "Login" page. Once there you will be asked to enter your registered email address. and submit the form. You will then receive a reset link in your email which you can follow to reset your password. Please remember that if you receive one such link and didn't request it then you must simply ignore it as this may have either been a system error or a fraud attempt.

If you have forgotten your email address then please use the link provided below the login form on the "Login" page. Once there you will then be asked to provide your registered phone number, a recovery email address and your password to verify your identity. If both values (phone number and password) are correct then your email address will be sent to you through the recovery password provided
