Welcome To Always On Point

Join a large community of artists ranging from musicians to authors and everything in between. Upload and share your talents with the world. What are you waiting for ?  

About Always On Point


Always On Point is a company that aims to provide artists with a platform to share their talents regardless of what those may be. If you're a musician, author, videographer, or photographer who is looking to gain exposure then this is the place for you

Create an account today 

It's quick and simple. All you have to do is create a free account using the button below, confirm your email address, and you're all set. You will then have access to our full library of uploads as well as uploading your own content. Follow other users, share, and like or favourite the posts you enjoy. 

How it works

Register and Sign-In

Before you will be allowed to upload content, like content, share, or follow users you will be required to register a free account. When doing this you will also need to specifiy whether you're an artist or a viewer, and what type of artist you are if the former option is selected.. You can then sign in and enjoy. Using the site without an account limits you to simply viewing content.

Create your first post

If you are an artist you will be able to upload content, however, the type of content is limited to the artist category you have selected, for example, musicians upload the audio file and an image thumbnail for that upload. 

Follow others like you

Explore the work uploaded by other artists on the website and if you find something you like then you can hit the "follow" button to automatically have all their new uploads pushed to your feed.

Share

Share content over social media to gain further exposure
